## 🧑‍🎓 About Me

I am **Rongsheng (Royce) Zhang**, an undergraduate researcher at the **University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC)**, double majoring in **Mathematics** and **Statistics** with a **minor in Computer Science**.
